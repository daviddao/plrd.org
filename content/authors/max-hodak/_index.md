Max is the Founder and CEO of Science Corporation, focused on restoring vision, cognition, and mobility. Science's PRIMA retina implant has completed pivotal clinical trials with over 80% of patients achieving significant visual acuity improvements—the first BCI technology to restore functional vision. Science's biohybrid BCI uses living neurons to connect to the brain, circumventing the constraints of traditional neural interfaces.

Prior to founding Science, Max was president and co-founder of Neuralink (2016) and co-founded Transcriptic, a robotic cloud laboratory for the life sciences.
